The first leadership development program dedicated to school principals, which brings together business managers, international professionals in education and the most relevant Romanian NGOs.

How can you get involved?

There are two ways through which you can participate in this project: you can either become a mentor or a coach or you can help offer a scholarship for a school principle. See below more info on each option.

1. Become a mentor or a coach for a school principal that takes part in The Leadership Academy

Who will benefit if you get involved?

School principlas from all arounc Romania, who submitted an application and was admitted in this 18 months change management program.

What do you have to do?

  • You will help with the training and transformation of the school principles that participate in the program.
  • You will contribute with your expertise and experience of at least 10 years in leadership positions.
  • You will make a commitment for approximately 18 months, which will start in Q3 of 2021, after the Ministry-organized contest which selects school principles for the next 4 years.
  • You will have minimum 10 mentoring meetings with the school principal during the entire program, recommended 60-90 min every 3-4 weeks.

What does a business-mentor’s profile look like?

You need to be a manager or higher with a minimum of 10 years’ leadership experience, and be open to share your experience with the participants. Curricula will be provided by AVE, and you will discuss with a manager of the program.

Find out more here.



2. Offer a scholarship for a school principles that takes part in The Leadership Academy

If you cannot get involved as a mentor, but still want to help, you may do so by supporting our crowdfunding initiative for a total amount of EUR 2000, which will help one school principal take part in the program. You can read more about it here. If you want to donate, make sure you complete this form.

Find out more here.

First integrated intervention platform, where key education-focused NGOs work together, for a period of at least 4 years in the same 114 schools, with the support of the Business Community.

Who will benefit if you get involved?

Every stakeholder in the education community – students, teachers, directors, communities. However, if we were to sum it up, the most important beneficiary would be students – the ultimate beneficiary who need this methodological change in education most.

What will you have to do?

You have three options, that are listed below:

  1. You can financially support a school from the Edu Networks program – per year, the cost would be of 15K EUR to support one school in all its necessities (Literacy and Critical Thinking & Integrated Digital Program & Professional Learning Communities for Teachers & The Leadership Academy for school principals) . Even though that sounds like a high enough number, but when you put it into perspective, it can mean if not dozens, hundreds of children. Therefore the narrative is that little for an education that will have a lifetime impact. Read more about the program here.
  2. You can donate IT hardware for schools: it is an easy process and AVE will ensure that those tools will be received by the students who need it most. In short, you would be offering access to education in a box. Here is the step-by-step procedure.
  3. You can share your knowledge via in-kind professional services: legal, IT, communication, financial, fiscal, funds, training, audit. Think of it as a knowledge transfer that will help its recepients benefit from the information they need to ensure that education is properly done. Here are all the areas you can contribute to. If you want to join, please complete the form below.

What do you need in order to qualify?

Nothing, really. For the monetary and IT donations, the links above will guide you through the process. For the in-kind donations, you only need to complete this form and be excited to start! An AVE representative will be contacting you soon.

Who will benefit if you get involed?

Children of all ages. At present, for every 3 months in which children are not in school, 4 years later they are still 1 and ½ years behind. The platform Narada is putting together will allow any school in Romania to sign on and signal an emergency that they have. Last year’s impact counted 620k children, for whom Narada faciliated access to education. Two points stand out: firstly, from a technological perspective, it helps Narada scale their initiative. Secondly, they deal with emergencies in an agile way, when we find out about the stringent needs of a community. It is also a means to communciate with the local authorities on matters pertaining to education, and it allows a buffer period for the authorities to step in.

You can firstly donate, as the cost of putting together such a big data platform raises to approximately 30k EUR. The crowdfunding website can be found here. Secondly, you can get involved in the platform’s design and organization. You can also spread the word and reach out to as many stakeholders as you want. You would be helping in colnsolidating the biggest educational platform in the country. Please find here more info.
